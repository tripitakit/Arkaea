Hi Claude,
I want to develop with you a project for a simulation game about the evolution of proto-bacterial organisms.
It must be scientifically accurate
It must be a WEB application with 2D WebGL UI
The player creates an Arkeon by defining the cellular structure and its genome.
The cellular structure has membrane and wall, with proteins that qualify its functions of interaction with the environment.
The genome must contain the genes for the structural and enzymatic proteins fundamental for the metabolism and replication of the cell.
The simulation must allow for the existence of mobile elements (such as plasmids or prophages) that can transfer phenotypic or metabolic traits, or antibiotic resistances.
The game must simplify by several orders of magnitude the real complexity of even a simple bacterial genome, but without trivializing the subject matter. The players will probably be biologists, microbiologists, geneticists, molecular biologists....
I need to start a brainstorming session with you to better define the scope of the project.
